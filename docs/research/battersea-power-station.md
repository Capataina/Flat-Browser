## Tags to Remove or Correct

- **Status: Consider adding "In delivery" alongside "Ready now"** — While 2,200+ homes are complete and the destination is fully operational, ~50% of the site is still undeveloped. The Gehry buildings and final masterplan phase won't complete until late 2020s/early 2030s. Both tags could apply, or the status could be read as "Ready now" since there is substantial occupied, functioning community already.
- **Demographic: "European-led" can be retained** but note that Census data reflects an extremely young community (population grew 14x between 2011-2021). "East Asian & European" was suggested due to Malaysian developers, but Census 2021 shows Asian at only 16.2% — insufficient to justify a dual tag. The Census data does not support changing to "East Asian & European".
- **Age: "30-40" is borderline** — ward-level data shows 20-29 slightly ahead of 30-39. The tag is defensible given BPS's premium positioning but not strongly Census-backed.

## Summary

Battersea Power Station's core tags are well-supported: Zone 1, rent or buy, waterside destination, and area/masterplan are all verified. The pricing tag "Mid-range, Premium" accurately reflects the range across phases. The main nuance is status: "Ready now" is accurate for the current community but the masterplan is clearly still in delivery with ~50% of the site remaining. The demographic tag "European-led" is supported by Census 2021 (58.4% White), and the suggestion to change to "East Asian & European" is not supported by Census data. Connectivity is Well Connected at ~25 minutes average.
