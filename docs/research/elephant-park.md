## Current state and liveability

Elephant Park is a nearly complete Zone 1 masterplan with strong connectivity (Bakerloo and Northern lines), a genuine two-acre central park, and an activated ground-floor retail offer. The final phase (The Wilderly) topped out in June 2025 and is on track for 2026 completion. The wider masterplan completion target is 2028. Census 2021 confirms the area is genuinely ethnically mixed, with substantial White (42.7%) and Black (34.6%) populations. Pricing sits in the mid-range for Zone 1 new-build, with rental entry around GBP 2,383 pcm. The "In delivery" and "Ready now" statuses could both apply — most of the development is complete and occupied.
