## Tags to Remove or Correct

- **Correct `Buy only` → `Rent or buy`**: Both official Battersea Power Station lettings pages and Rightmove show active Switch House rental listings from studios to 4-bed apartments. Sale listings are also active. The live market is clearly rent-or-buy.
- **Remove `European-led`**: Census 2021 Nine Elms ward data shows only 30% White British in a diverse area. The developer consortium is Malaysian. No building-level resident demographic data is available. "European-led" is not substantiated.
- **Remove `40+`**: The Nine Elms ward is dominated by 20–39 year olds. While the ultra-prime price point and heritage scarcity may attract wealthier, older buyers, this inference cannot be retained under Census-only evidence rules.

## Developer Note

The developer is listed as "SP Setia - Sime Darby - EPF" which refers to the Malaysian-led consortium (SP Setia Bhd, Sime Darby Property, and the Employees Provident Fund of Malaysia). The wider development is managed by Battersea Power Station Development Company Limited, which is active on Companies House (company 08150687). This is accurate.
