1. **Rental qualification details** — Almost no operator publicly lists income multiples, visa policies, guarantor requirements, or credit check procedures on their websites. Every building marked "unknown — needs direct enquiry" for graduate-visa realism requires a phone call or email to the lettings team.

2. **Exact unit counts** — Many BTR operators do not publish precise unit numbers for individual buildings. Fizzy Living is particularly opaque.

3. **Resident reviews** — HomeViews coverage is patchy for newer buildings. Trustpilot reviews exist for UNCLE (mixed — some management complaints) but not systematically for others.

4. **Architect attribution** — Many mid-market BTR buildings do not prominently credit their architects.

### Priority direct enquiries for Caner's situation

Given the graduate-visa, no-payslips, upfront-rent constraint set:

1. **The Maple (Brent Cross Town)** — BTR with pool, lowest Zone 3 price floor in dataset. Ask about visa-friendly referencing.
2. **Enclave Croydon** — No deposit, all-inclusive model. Ask about income/visa requirements.
3. **UNCLE Stockwell** — Lowest UNCLE price point (£1,480 pcm). Ask about visa-friendly referencing.
4. **UNCLE Wembley** — £1,795 pcm, Zone 4 Wembley. Ask about referencing.
5. **Moda Embassy Boulevard** — Progressive branding but premium pricing. Ask about non-traditional income proofs.
6. **Grainger Fortunes Dock** — Institutional operator, Canning Town. Ask about referencing flexibility.
7. **Fizzy Canning Town** — Greystar-owned, Zone 3. Ask about visa and referencing.
8. **Apo Kew Bridge** — Membership model may be different. Ask specifically how membership qualification works for visa holders.

### Areas needing deeper project discovery

- **Croydon** — The Amro Partners BTR (planning secured) needs timeline tracking. One Lansdowne (tall tower, planning stage) could be significant if it delivers.
- **Woolwich** — The Maribor/Ropeyards scheme needs monitoring (completion 2029). Flora Apartments needs detail.
- **Thamesmead** — Peabody's Southmere + Lesnes Estate pipeline (up to 2,142 homes) could make the Thamesmead edge a real contender, especially with Abbey Wood Elizabeth Line access.
- **Canada Water** — Next residential phase (post-revised masterplan approval) needs tracking. Start on site expected 2027.
- **Royal Docks** — Ballymore's Thames Road (1,685 homes) and Knights Road (1,667 homes) are pipeline-transforming if they deliver.
