## Data gaps and research limitations

1. **Rent prices** -- Many developments do not publish rental prices publicly. The prices listed are from agent listings at a point in time and may not reflect current availability.
2. **Visa-friendliness** -- No developer explicitly advertises "visa-friendly" policies. BTR operators (Greystar, Get Living, L&G Living, Fizzy) are generally more flexible than private landlords, but this is inferred rather than confirmed.
3. **Income multiples and upfront policies** -- These are rarely published for BTR schemes and need direct inquiry to confirm.
4. **Referencing providers** -- Most BTR operators use in-house referencing or partners like Goodlord/Canopy; specifics not available from web searches.
5. **HomeViews scores** -- Not systematically checked in this sweep. Recommend a focused HomeViews sweep for all projects.
6. **Herne Hill** -- This area has no managed rental buildings of any kind. The area may not be suitable for the Flatbrowser dataset unless the scope includes Victorian conversion flats (which it does not).
7. **Tooting** -- No dedicated BTR schemes found. Springfield Place is the main development but is build-to-sell.
