# East London Rental Projects — Research Report

Research date: 2026-04-12
Scope: 11 East London areas, focused on managed rental buildings (BTR, PRS, housing-association-managed, developer-operated lettings)

---

## How to read this

Each project entry records what could be verified from public sources. Fields marked `unknown` need further investigation — typically by contacting the operator directly or checking the operator's portal. Rental qualification details (income multiples, upfront policy, visa-friendliness, referencing provider) are rarely published and almost always require direct enquiry.

**Building type key:** BTR = Build-to-Rent (purpose-built for renting, single institutional landlord). PRS = Private Rented Sector (managed lettings but not purpose-built BTR). Mixed = contains both for-sale and rental units. BtS = Build-to-Sell (primarily sales, but units appear on the rental market via individual landlords).

## Key Findings for Caner's Search

### Strongest BTR candidates (most likely visa-friendly, professional management)

1. **Fizzy Stepney Green** (Mile End area) — Greystar operator, award-winning, modest amenities but professional management
2. **Fizzy Poplar** (Poplar) — Greystar operator, 1-bed from £2,155/mo, near DLR
3. **Fizzy East 16** (Canning Town) — Greystar operator, 292 units, 25-storey towers
4. **Argo Apartments** (Canning Town) — Grainger plc, 1-bed from £2,070/mo, part of Hallsville Quarter
5. **The Altham** (Walthamstow) — Morro operator, studios with bills included, flexible tenancies
6. **The Eades** (Walthamstow) — Way of Life operator, strong amenity package
7. **Dalston Works** (Dalston) — Dedicated BTR, world's largest CLT building, Conran interiors
8. **Stratford Mill** (Stratford) — Greystar BTR, completing Autumn 2026
9. **Manhattan Loft Gardens** (Stratford) — 248 rental lofts by SOM, premium

### Pricing observations

- Most affordable managed options: Rathbone East (Canning Town, 1-bed from £1,375), Argo Apartments (Canning Town, 1-bed from £2,070)
- Walthamstow cluster: Blackhorse Mills + The Altham + The Eades + Blackhorse View creates a dense BTR corridor along the Victoria Line
- Premium: Alma (Whitechapel, studio from £2,950), London Dock (Wapping, 1-bed from £2,400+), Manhattan Loft Gardens (Stratford, 1-bed from £2,600+)

### Gaps in coverage

- **Bethnal Green** has essentially zero BTR options — Broadway East is sales-led and still completing
- **Hackney Central** is underserved — mostly council-led development, only one small private development (Hackney Gardens, 58 units)
- **Wapping** has no genuine BTR — Sovereign Court (PRS, 124 units) is the closest, rest is individual-landlord market
- **Mile End** has one Fizzy property at the edge (Stepney Green) but no BTR within the core area; Bow Green will be massive but sales-led

### Areas with strongest BTR density

1. **Canning Town** — Fizzy Canning Town (existing) + Fizzy East 16 + Argo Apartments + Rathbone Market + Rathbone East
2. **Walthamstow (Blackhorse Road)** — Blackhorse Mills (existing) + The Altham + The Eades + Blackhorse View
3. **Stratford** — East Village (existing) + Manhattan Loft Gardens + Stratford Mill + Stratford Studios
4. **Poplar** — London City Island (existing) + Fizzy Poplar + Blackwall Reach (HA)
