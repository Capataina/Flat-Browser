## Tags to Add

- **connectivity: Well Connected** — Brent Cross West (Thameslink) to King's Cross: ~12 min direct. To Soho (via KX + Piccadilly/Northern): ~24 min. To Shoreditch (via KX + overground/tube): ~32 min. To Canary Wharf (via KX + Northern/Jubilee): ~38 min. Average: ~27 min.
- Consider adding **status: "Ready now"** alongside "In delivery" — The Maple and The Delamarre are both marketing homes as "available to move in now" per the official site, even though the wider masterplan is still building out.

## Tags to Remove or Correct

- **zone: Remove "Zone 4"** — Both Brent Cross West (Thameslink) and Brent Cross Underground (Northern line) are Zone 3. No Zone 4 station serves the development.
- **demo: Change "European-led" → "Mixed"** — Cricklewood ward Census 2021 data shows White at 43.4%, with significant Arab (10%), Black African (11%), and Asian (9%+) populations. The area does not have a European-led demographic profile.
- **age: Remove "30-40" or flag as unverified** — Census 2021 proxy data shows 40-49 as the largest decade group, not 30-39. The age distribution is very flat across working-age cohorts. The scheme is too new for resident-specific age data.

## Summary

Brent Cross Town's core proposition — an affordable-to-mid-range parkside masterplan with good Thameslink connectivity — is well-verified. The main corrections needed are: (1) Zone 4 should be removed (both stations are Zone 3), (2) the demographic tag should change from "European-led" to "Mixed" based on Cricklewood ward Census data showing genuine diversity with no ethnic majority, and (3) the age mix tag lacks Census support and should be removed or flagged. The development is genuinely in delivery with first residents since March 2025.

## Confidence: Medium-High

Zone, tenure, price, status, character, community, and place type tags are strongly supported. The demographic correction is well-evidenced by Census 2021 ward data. Age mix confidence is low due to the flat age distribution in the proxy ward and the absence of scheme-specific resident data.
